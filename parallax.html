COFORD Council

The COFORD Council for Forest Research and Development is a representative body from the Forest Sector in Ireland. The Council advises the Department of Agriculture Food and the Marine on research and developmental issues.

Learn more

Tools and Services

COFORD organises a variety of events each year, including workshops, seminars and conferences. Some of these are co-hosted with other organisations.

Research Programme

The COFORD research programme is the responsibility of the Research, Food and CODEX Co-ordination Division of the Department of Agriculture, Food and the Marine.
